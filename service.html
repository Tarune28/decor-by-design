Services provided here

We provide a variety of services for all needs!

At Decorative design, no event is small or big! All events decorations are custom designed to your needs, taste and budget. Careful selection of materials, budget friendly packages and high-quality designs are some of the key features which will amaze you and your guests. We can do just the backdrop decoration or we can do the entire event decoration; either way, you and your guests are sure to be amazed.

We also do custom wall murals, with popular requests being children's rooms.



Event Decoration Services:


Birthday Parties

When we plan birthday's, our goal is to make it as memorable as possible. Paying extra attention to detail gives it the Decorative Design touch. We've helped decorate several birthday events—all of them resulting in stunning events. Choose Decorative Design for your next birthday!

Graduation Ceremony

With graduation ceremonies being a once in a lifetime opportunity, Decorative Design plans unique decorations for the event. We use some of our best decorations and planning efforts to make the day as special as possible.

Baby Showers

We decorate baby shower events, giving each customer a personalized experience for their special day. All of our decorations are tailored specifically for this event. Enjoy your baby shower with the elegant decorations we have to offer.

Bridal Showers

With affordable rates and high quality, choosing Decorative Design for your Bridal shower results in stunning events. We take the time to make sure every detail is perfect! Our dedication and work ethic help put together brilliant bridal showers.

Engagement Ceremonies

We take extra effort and preparation to make your engagement ceremony as special as possible. Celebrate the day with Decorative Design's stunning event planning. We decorate all elements of the event and more to ensure our clients can enjoy a beautiful engagement ceremony!

Wall Mural painting

Wall Mural paintings are the easiest way to transform a place from ordinary into something extraordinary! Our most popular requests are to do murals for kid’s bedrooms. At Decorative Design, we take your ideas, process your thoughts and shape your dream with details because in your dream every single detail matters! We can do 2D and 3D murals. With our 3D murals, we even try to incorporate the client's personal belongings especially when we do projects for kids’ bedrooms.

Giant paper flower designs

Giant paper flowers are used as a backdrop in all types of parties and also at homes as a decoration. With our creativity, a simple paper can be turned into a work of art. These are very inexpensive yet most rich and elegant looking decorations. These paper flowers can be made in different colors, sizes, shapes and designs. These are handmade and individually crafted according to clients’ needs.

Balloon Arches and Garlands for Parties

What a party without BALLOONS! They are fun, colorful and vibrant. At Decorative Design, we make all types of balloons decorations, balloon arches, balloon columns and balloon garlands. We also do themed party decorations with our balloons for all age groups and it sure does create a memorable day for them especially our young clients as little as 2 years old! Please contact us for price information as each project is tailored to meet your needs.
